Hello, I'm Leana Wong

"That's so cuteeee!" We have all roamed the supermarket sometime in our lives and had our attention captured by the packaging of an instant noodle packet, a snack, a drink, or by one of the various other endless products down the aisle. Well... maybe that was overgeneralizing that "we have all" done that, but I am sure at least I have.


I grew up going on grocery trips with my mom, and while she would walk around the produce section searching for the freshest veggies at their best values, I would wander off to the snacks and juice section. Amidst being drawn to the "on sale" signs, I would be distracted by all the packaging I found attractive. "Ooooh that color's so pretty! WAAAIT that's the cutest little mascot! Hmm I can't really read what the flavor is... :O is that a cat?!?"


Humans are innately visual creatures, so I want to take advantage of that. I aspire to become a packaging designer who uses my design and storytelling skills to influence consumers to choose healthier and more sustainable options. I am currently pursuing a Bachelors of Arts degree in Speculative Design and minoring in Cognitive Science at the University of California, San Diego.
